### Tower Starting Stats

*   **Max Health:** 20
*   **Base Damage:** 5
*   **Attack Range:** 230
*   **Attack Cooldown:** 1000 ms (1 sec)
*   **Health Regen:** -0.3 HP/sec (natural drain)

---

### Implemented Neural Tree Nodes

#### Core / Startup
*   **AWAKEN**
    *   Description: Begin existence.
    *   Parent: None
    *   Children: COGNITION, INTEGRITY, INTENSITY
    *   Max Level: 1
    *   Cost: 0 Data

#### Cursor Attacks (Tier 1)
*   **COGNITION**
    *   Description: Your cursor now auto-attacks.
    *   *In-Depth:* Enables a square Area of Effect (AOE) attack centered on your mouse cursor. It fires automatically every 2000 ms (2 seconds). By default, it deals 4 damage to all enemies within a 100x100 pixel square radius.
    *   Parent: AWAKEN
    *   Children: AMPLIFY, ATTRACT, LIGHTNING, SHOCKWAVE
    *   Max Level: 1
    *   Cost: 1 Data
*   **AMPLIFY**
    *   Description: +2 cursor damage
    *   Parent: COGNITION
    *   Children: EXPANSION
    *   Max Level: 3
    *   Cost: 5 Data (Linear +10 per level)
*   **EXPANSION**
    *   Description: +30% cursor attack size
    *   Parent: AMPLIFY
    *   Children: OVERCHARGE
    *   Max Level: 1
    *   Cost: 20 Data

#### Tower Upgrades (Tier 1)
*   **INTEGRITY**
    *   Description: +5 tower max health
    *   Parent: AWAKEN
    *   Children: REGEN, ARMOR
    *   Max Level: 4
    *   Cost: 4 Data (Linear +8 per level)
*   **INTENSITY**
    *   Description: +2 tower basic damage
    *   Parent: AWAKEN
    *   Children: INFLUENCE
    *   Max Level: 5
    *   Cost: 5 Data (Linear +5 per level)
*   **INFLUENCE**
    *   Description: +20% tower attack range
    *   Parent: INTENSITY
    *   Children: None
    *   Max Level: 1
    *   Cost: 20 Data
*   **REGEN**
    *   Description: +0.2 health regen
    *   Parent: INTEGRITY
    *   Children: None
    *   Max Level: 3
    *   Cost: 6 Data (Linear +6 per level)
*   **ARMOR**
    *   Description: Reduces incoming damage by 1.
    *   Parent: INTEGRITY
    *   Children: None
    *   Max Level: 3
    *   Cost: 10 Data (Linear +10 per level)

#### Utility (Tier 1)
*   **ATTRACT**
    *   Description: +40% resource pickup range
    *   Parent: COGNITION
    *   Children: None
    *   Max Level: 1
    *   Cost: 1 Insight

#### Tier 1 Duo-Box (Select One)
*   **LIGHTNING** (Duo-Box Option A)
    *   Description: Tower gains a lightning weapon that chains across enemies.
    *   *In-Depth:* Fires a jagged lightning bolt from the tower to the nearest enemy every 2500 ms (2.5 seconds). It deals a base of 6 damage and will automatically chain to 1 additional enemy within 115 pixels of the primary target.
    *   Parent: COGNITION
    *   Children: FORK, VOLTAGE
    *   Max Level: 1
    *   Cost: 1 Shard
    *   *Sub-nodes:*
        *   **FORK:** +1 lightning chain target
            *   Parent: LIGHTNING
            *   Children: None
            *   (Max Lvl 1, 15 Data)
        *   **VOLTAGE:** +50% lightning damage
            *   Parent: LIGHTNING
            *   Children: None
            *   (Max Lvl 1, 20 Data)

*   **SHOCKWAVE** (Duo-Box Option B)
    *   Description: Tower pulses a shockwave every 3s, damaging nearby enemies.
    *   *In-Depth:* Creates an expanding circular ring centered on the tower every 3000 ms (3 seconds). It deals a base of 6 damage to all enemies caught within its 160 pixel radius.
    *   Parent: COGNITION
    *   Children: AMPLIFIER, RESONANCE
    *   Max Level: 1
    *   Cost: 1 Shard
    *   *Sub-nodes:*
        *   **AMPLIFIER:** +25% shockwave damage & range
            *   Parent: SHOCKWAVE
            *   Children: None
            *   (Max Lvl 1, 40 Data)
        *   **RESONANCE:** Shockwave deals +1/2/3 dmg per enemy hit
            *   Parent: SHOCKWAVE
            *   Children: None
            *   (Max Lvl 3, 30 Data, Linear +30)
