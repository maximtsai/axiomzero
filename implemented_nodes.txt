# AXIOM ZERO — IMPLEMENTED NODES SUMMARY

This document tracks all currently implemented nodes in the Neural Tree, including their IDs, costs, and primary effects.

---

## TIER 0: THE SEED
- **AWAKEN** (`awaken`)
  - **Cost:** 0 DATA
  - **Effect:** Initializes the tower and reveals the initial upgrade path.

---

## TIER 1: CORE SYSTEMS

### Foundation
- **INTEGRITY** (`integrity`)
  - **Cost:** 4 DATA (Scales linearly)
  - **Effect:** Increases tower Max Health.
- **INTENSITY** (`intensity`)
  - **Cost:** 5 DATA (Scales linearly)
  - **Effect:** Increases tower Basic Attack Damage.
- **COGNITION** (`basic_pulse`)
  - **Cost:** 1 DATA
  - **Effect:** Unlocks Cursor Pulse Attack.

### Cursor Path
- **CONCENTRATION** (`pulse_damage`)
  - **Cost:** 5 DATA (Scales linearly)
  - **Effect:** Increases Cursor Pulse Damage.
- **EXPANSION** (`pulse_expansion`)
  - **Cost:** 40 DATA
  - **Effect:** Increases Cursor Pulse Size. Requires Max Concentration.
- **PACKET SNIFFING** (`packet_sniffing`)
  - **Cost:** 1 INSIGHT
  - **Effect:** Unlocks Data Sniffing (resource bonuses).

### Utility & Survival
- **CONVERGENCE** (`magnet`)
  - **Cost:** 1 INSIGHT
  - **Effect:** Increases Data Pickup Range.
- **RECOVERY** (`regen`)
  - **Cost:** 10 DATA (Scales linearly)
  - **Effect:** Counteracts negative health regen.
- **SECURITY** (`armor`)
  - **Cost:** 50 DATA
  - **Effect:** Adds Flat Armor reduction.
- **RECOVERY PROTOCOL** (`threat_response`)
  - **Cost:** 150 DATA
  - **Effect:** Heals 30% of missing HP when a Boss appears.

### Economy
- **CRYPTO MINE** (`crypto_mine_unlock`)
  - **Cost:** 0 DATA
  - **Effect:** Unlocks passive Netcoin generation.
- **DATA COMPRESSION** (`data_compression`)
  - **Cost:** 2 INSIGHT
  - **Effect:** Reveals advanced tech branches (Tier 2 tech).

### DUO-BOX 1: WEAPON SYSTEMS
- **LIGHTNING** (`lightning_weapon`)
  - **Cost:** 1 SHARD
  - **Choice:** Chains across targets.
- **SHOCKWAVE** (`shockwave_weapon`)
  - **Cost:** 1 SHARD
  - **Choice:** Pulses AoE damage around the tower.

---

## TIER 2: ADVANCED PROTOCOLS

### Active Tech
- **OVERCHARGE** (`overcharge`)
  - **Cost:** 60 DATA
  - **Effect:** Massive boost to Cursor Damage.
- **OVERCLOCK** (`overclock`)
  - **Cost:** 50 DATA (Scales linearly)
  - **Effect:** Reduces ability cooldowns.

### DUO-BOX 2: PULSE SPECIALIZATION
- **MANUAL PROTOCOL** (`manual_pulse`)
  - **Cost:** 1 SHARD
  - **Choice:** Disables auto-pulse; grants manual click-to-fire charges.
- **RESONANCE AREA** (`wide_pulse`)
  - **Cost:** 1 SHARD
  - **Choice:** Massive increase to Cursor Pulse area.

---

## LORE ARCHIVE
- **ARCHIVE I–IX** (`lore_1` through `lore_9`)
  - **Cost:** 1 DATA each
  - **Effect:** Unlocks sequential narrative fragments regarding the AI's origin.
