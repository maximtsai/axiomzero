# Axiom Zero - Gameplay and Node Ideas

## 1. Incremental Economy & Flow
- **Turbo / Overclock Mode:** A "Call Wave Early" toggle for when the player's build is steamrolling. Increases spawn rate but rewards extra resources.
- **Node Cost Scaling:** Apply `costStepScaling` to high-value linear nodes (e.g., Overclock, Prismatic Array) to maintain the economy long-term.
- **Goal:** Maintains the challenge-to-growth ratio.
- **Note:** This is an incremental game, not an idle one. No offline progress is planned.

## 2. Information & Stats (Buyable Node)
- **Concept:** An upgrade node that unlocks "Diagnostic Analytics."
- **Feature:** Shows a percentage breakdown of Damage Sources (Pulse, Lightning, etc.) on the **End of Iteration report.**
- **Goal:** Helps players understand which build specialization is carrying their performance.

## 3. Visual & Audio "Juice" Scaling
- **Concept:** Stats should have a visible and audible impact on the game's presentation.
- **Milestone Idea:** High fire rates create a continuous white-hot muzzle glow.
- **Milestone Idea:** Damage intensity scales screen-shake and bass depth.
- **Visual Clarity:** "Damage Number Compression" - Combine many fast small hits into a single "Totalizing" number that updates rapidly.

## 4. Cursor & Tower Synergies (The "Target Paint" System)
- **Concept:** Cursor hits "Mark" enemies.
- **Node Idea:** "Signal Boost" - Marked enemies take +50% total damage from the tower for 2 seconds.
- **Goal:** Strategic target selection during intense boss phases.

## 5. New Enemy Types (Tactical Variety)
- **The "Data Chest":** Zero-threat, high-HP "loot goblin" for massive shard bursts.
- **The "Shielded" Enemy:** Has a one-time hit shield that absorbs **any** single amount of damage. 
- **Shielded Miniboss:** Extends the shield mechanic to a more dangerous threat, forcing the player to waste a shot or use a high fire-rate weapon to break the protection.
- **Goal:** Breaks raw DPS "steamrolling" by requiring multiple hits regardless of damage numbers.

## 6. Emergency & Panic Protocols (Active Survival)
- **Concept:** High-cooldown manual abilities.
- **Node Idea:** "System Flush" (EMP screen-clear) and "Hardened Shell" (temporary hit-absorption shield).
- **Goal:** Clutch player agency in high-stress combat phases.

## 7. Build Specializations (Duo Capstones)
- **Concept:** Mutually exclusive Capstone nodes.
- **Goal:** Unique build identities per iteration.
